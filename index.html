THE ARCHITECT OF SILENCE

Semiyo

A keeper of silent promises

Semiyo expresses his heart not in grand declarations, but through subtle strokes of his pen and quiet, unwavering presence. Left behind in the quiet wake of Oliviya's departure, his entries reflect a soul trying to preserve a love that walked away, seeking solace in the ocean waves.

"If love is a silent storm, then I am content to be drowned in your rain."
THE KEEPER OF MEMORIES

Oliviya

A heart woven out of poetry

Oliviya is a vibrant collage of emotions, painting the canvas of her life with words, songs, and wild blossoms. As the one who chose to walk away, her diary entries are beautiful, vulnerable chronicles of why she had to leave, the ache of departure, and the memories she still holds close.

"My diary is the only place where you and I still walk under the golden sun, untroubled by tomorrow."
